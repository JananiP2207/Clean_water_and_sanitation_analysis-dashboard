The Clean Water and Sanitation Dashboard provides a comprehensive visual analysis of water quality, access to clean water, sanitation, and public health trends, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation).
This dashboard highlights global and regional challenges in water access and their impact on health outcomes.

**Objectives:**

Monitor diarrheal disease trends over time

Analyze water source distribution

Compare clean water access across countries and regions

Track pH level variations over the years
